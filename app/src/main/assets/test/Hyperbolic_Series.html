Series for hyperbolic functions

$$ \sinh x = x + \frac{x^3}{3!} + \frac{x^5}{5!} + \cdot \cdot \cdot $$ $$ \cosh x = 1 + \frac{x^2}{2!} + \frac{x^4}{4!} + \cdot \cdot \cdot $$ $$ \tanh x = x - \frac{x^3}{3} + \frac{2x^5}{15} + \cdot \cdot \cdot \frac{(-1)^{n-1} 2^{2n}\left(2^{2n}-1\right)B_nx^{2n-1}}{(2n)!} + \cdot \cdot \cdot \quad |x| < \frac{\pi}{2} $$ $$ \coth x = \frac{1}{x} + \frac{x}{3} - \frac{x^3}{45} + \cdot \cdot \cdot \frac{(-1)^{n-1} 2^{2n}B_nx^{2n-1}}{(2n)!} + \cdot \cdot \cdot \quad 0 < |x| < \pi $$
