Definitions and properties:

Second derivative: $$ \frac{d^2}{dx^2}\left(f(x)\right)\ = \frac{d}{dx} \left(\frac{dy}{dx}\right) - \frac{d^2y}{dx^2} $$ Higher-Order derivative: $$ \frac{d^n}{dx^n}\left(f(x)\right)\ = \frac{d}{dx}\left( \frac{d^\left(n-1\right)}{dx^\left(n-1\right)}\left(f(x)\right) \right)\ $$ $$ \frac{d^n}{dx^n}\left(f(x) \, \pm \, g(x)\right) = \frac{d^n}{dx^n}f(x) \pm \frac{d^n}{dx^n}~g(x) $$ Leibniz's Formulas: $$ \frac{d^2}{dx^2}(f(x) \cdot g(x)) = g(x)\cdot\frac{d^2}{dx^2}f(x) + 2 \cdot \frac{d}{dx}f(x)\cdot \frac{d}{dx}g(x) + f(x) \cdot \frac{d^2}{dx^2}g(x)$$ $$ \frac{d^3}{dx^3}(f(x) \cdot g(x)) = g(x)\cdot \frac{d^3}{dx^3}f(x) + 3 \cdot \frac{d^2}{dx^2}f(x)\cdot \frac{d}{dx}g(x) + 3 \cdot \frac{d}{dx}f(x)\cdot \frac{d^2}{dx^2}g(x) + f(x) \cdot \frac{d^3}{dx^3}g(x)$$ $$ \frac{d^n}{dx^n}(f \cdot g) = g \cdot \frac{d^n}{dx^n}f + n \cdot \frac{d^{(n-1)}}{dx^{(n-1)}}f\cdot \frac{d}{dx}g + \frac{n(n-1)}{1\cdot2} \cdot \frac{d^{(n-2)}}{dx^{(n-2)}}f \cdot \frac{d^2}{dx^2}g + \dots + f \cdot \frac{d^n}{dx^n}g $$

Important Formulas:

$$ \left(x^m \right)^{(n)} = \frac{ m! }{(m-n)!} x^{m-n} $$ $$ \left( x^n \right)^{(n)} = n! $$ $$ \left( \log_a x \right)^{(n)} = \frac{(-1)^{(n-1)} \cdot (n-1)!}{x^n \cdot \ln a} $$ $$ (\ln n)^{(n)} = \frac{(-1)^{n-1}(n-1)!}{x^n} $$ $$ \left( a^x \right)^{(n)} = a^x \cdot \ln^n a $$ $$ \left( e^x \right)^{(n)} = e^x $$ $$ \left( a^{m \, x} \right)^{(n)} = m^n \, a^{m \cdot x} \ln^n a $$ $$ (\sin x)^{(n)} = \sin\left(x + \frac{n\,\pi}{2} \right) $$ $$ (\cos x)^{(n)} = \cos\left(x + \frac{n\,\pi}{2} \right) $$
