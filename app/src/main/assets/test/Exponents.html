Exponential Formulas

$$ a^p = a \cdot a \cdot \dots a ~~~( \text{if} ~~ p \in \mathbb{N} ) $$ $$ a^0 = 1 ~~~ (\text{if} ~~~ a \ne 0) $$ $$ a^r \cdot a^s = a^{r+s} $$ $$ \frac{a^r}{a^s} = a^{r-s} $$ $$ \left( a^r\right)^s = a^{r \cdot s} $$ $$ (a \cdot b)^r = a^r \cdot b^r $$ $$ \left( \frac{a}{b} \right)^r = \frac{a^r}{b^r} $$ $$ a^{-r} = \frac{1}{a^r} $$ $$ a^{\Large\frac{r}{s}} = \sqrt[\Large{s}]{a^r} $$
