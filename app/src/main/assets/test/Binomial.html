Binomial series

$$ \begin{aligned} (a + x)^n &= a^n + na^{n-1} + \frac{n(n-1)}{2!} a^{n-2}x^2 + \frac{n(n-1)(n-2)}{3!}a^{n-3}x^3+\cdot \cdot \cdot \\ &= a^n + { n \choose 1} a^{n-1}x + { n \choose 2} a^{n-2}x^2 + { n \choose 3} a^{n-3}x^3 + \cdot \cdot \cdot \end{aligned} $$

Special cases of binomial series

$$ (1 + x)^{-1} = 1 - x + x^2 -x^3 + \cdot \cdot \cdot \quad -1 < x < 1 $$ $$ (1 + x)^{-2} = 1 - 2x + 3x^2 - 4x^3 + \cdot \cdot \cdot \quad -1 < x < 1 $$ $$ (1 + x)^{-3} = 1 - 3x + 6x^2 - 10x^3 + \cdot \cdot \cdot \quad -1 < x < 1 $$ $$ (1 + x)^{-1/2} = 1 - \frac{1}{2}x + \frac{1\cdot 3}{2\cdot 4}x^2 - \frac{1\cdot 3 \cdot 5}{2\cdot 4 \cdot 6}x^3 + \cdot \cdot \cdot \quad -1 < x \leq 1 $$ $$ (1 + x)^{1/2} = 1 + \frac{1}{2}x - \frac{1}{2\cdot 4}x^2 + \frac{1\cdot 3}{2\cdot 4 \cdot 6}x^3 + \cdot \cdot \cdot \quad -1 < x \leq 1 $$
